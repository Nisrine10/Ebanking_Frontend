Types of Bank Accounts

Saving Account: A saving account is designed to help individuals save money over time. It usually offers interest on the deposited amount and allows limited withdrawals.

Current Account: A current account, also known as a checking account, is designed for daily transactions. It allows frequent deposits and withdrawals without restrictions.
